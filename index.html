ABOUT ME

My story begins much like that of any international spy... I don't know much about my past just that I woke-up on the shore of a beach with a nasty bump to my head.

I'd like to think I sustained my injuries while fighting an international terrorist group or while trying to gain intel on some drug lord, but most likely I was drunk on some Pabst Blue Ribbon and slipped off the side of a boat.

Life as a spy wasn't all that I thought it would be. It was nothing but long hours and crappy benefits. I decided to follow a dream and try out a career in pop music. It became a semi-successful with such top hit wonders such as:
