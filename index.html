Joyce Kwon

ABOUT ME

Joyce was raised an artist. A classical cellist and a pianist at a very young age, Joyce studied with one of the most well regarded names in the classical world, attending San Francisco Prep Conservatory, and now, New York University.

She found her new passion when she discovered the world of design. Dozens of posters, logos, magazine covers, and other works have molded her into an enthusiastic and versatile designer, whether working with startups, music festicals, or charity events.

Aside from music and design, Joyce enjoys snowboarding and speed skating.
